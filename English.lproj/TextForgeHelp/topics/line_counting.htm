Line / Position Counting

The line number in the bottom left of the window tells you what hard line you're on. A new hard line is created by inserting a line break into the document. If you have wrapping turned it may look like the cursor is on line 10 when in fact it is still on the first line (because its just wrapping). The position number tells you how many characters into a hard line you are.
