Reporting Bugs

Please, please, please report any bugs you find. I have worked very hard to get the bugs out of TextForge, and there's nothing I like more than squashing the ones the slip by me. Please send the following information when reporting a bug:

  • A very detailed description of how to reproduce the bug
  • Your OS version, and any modifications you have made to your OS and/or the Finder. This includes most Unsanity products, and products that "Theme" the finder
  • The type of computer you have (e.g. PowerBook G4/667 w/512mb RAM)
  • Any text files you think would be helpful in reproducing/illustrating the bug

Send this information to "christian@trancesoftware.com" - I will reply quickly and if the bug is really a bug he will fix it ASAP. Here's hoping you never find one ;-)
